Subspecialty Opportunities

Develop deep expertise in your chosen specialty:

❤️ Cardiac & Thoracic

Expert-led training in cardiac anaesthesia, transoesophageal echocardiography, and haemodynamic monitoring for complex cardiac and thoracic procedures.

🧠 Neuro-Anaesthesia

Neurosurgical anaesthesia with advanced neurophysiological monitoring, ICP management, and awake craniotomy techniques.

👶 Obstetric Anaesthesia

Obstetric anaesthesia including epidural analgesia, spinal techniques, and management of high-risk obstetric patients.

👧 Paediatric Anaesthesia

Specialised training in paediatric airway management, induction techniques, and perioperative care of neonates and children.

🩸 Transplant Anaesthesia

Liver, kidney, and heart transplant anaesthesia with expertise in complex haemodynamic management and organ preservation.

😌 Pain Management

Chronic pain management, interventional pain procedures, and multimodal analgesia strategies for complex pain syndromes.

🚑 Critical Care & ICU

Intensive care management with expertise in ARDS, sepsis, multi-organ failure, and advanced life support.

⚠️ Emergency Medicine

Emergency trauma anaesthesia, resuscitation protocols, and acute airway management with rapid decision-making skills.
