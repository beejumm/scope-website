Our Services

Comprehensive anaesthesia, critical care, and pain management solutions

Scope Anaesthesia Services delivers a full spectrum of perioperative care services covering all surgical disciplines. Our five-partner collaborative model ensures expertise, availability, and excellence across every subspecialty of anaesthesia and critical care.

💊

General Anaesthesia

All standard techniques for elective, emergency, and day-care procedures. Comprehensive induction, maintenance, and recovery protocols ensuring safe anaesthesia delivery across all patient populations.

💉

Regional Anaesthesia

Spinal, epidural, peripheral nerve blocks, and USG-guided blocks. Advanced regional techniques minimizing systemic drug exposure and enhancing post-operative analgesia.

❤️

Cardiac & Thoracic Anaesthesia

Specialized care for cardiac surgeries, CABG, valve replacements, and thoracic procedures. Advanced hemodynamic monitoring and management for complex cardiac cases.

🧠

Neuro-Anaesthesia

Brain and spine surgery anaesthesia with advanced neuromonitoring. Expertise in maintaining cerebral perfusion and ICP management during neurosurgical procedures.

👶

Obstetric Anaesthesia

High-risk pregnancy management, labour analgesia, and caesarean section anaesthesia. Safe care for mother and baby with expertise in emergency obstetric scenarios.

👧

Paediatric & Neonatal Anaesthesia

Specialized protocols for the youngest patients. Age-appropriate dosing, airway management, and perioperative care tailored to paediatric physiology.

🫀

Transplant Anaesthesia

Liver, cardiac, and organ transplant programs. Expertise in managing the unique perioperative challenges of transplant surgery and multi-organ procurement.

🚑

Critical Care / ICU

24/7 SICU and general ICU management with advanced monitoring. Comprehensive post-operative care, mechanical ventilation, and crisis management.

⚠️

Emergency Services

Trauma anaesthesia, CODE BLUE response, and mass casualty preparedness. Immediate availability and expertise for all emergency perioperative situations.

😌

Pain Management

Acute and chronic pain management, USG-guided procedures, and multimodal analgesia. Specialized pain relief strategies for optimal post-operative recovery.

🏃

Day Care & Ambulatory

Efficient anaesthesia for short-stay procedures. Rapid recovery protocols ensuring safe discharge and quick return to normal activities.

🛏️

Non-OR Anaesthesia

Sedation for endoscopy, cath lab, MRI, bronchoscopy, and ECT. Safe sedation and anaesthesia management outside the operating theatre environment.

✅

Perioperative Services

Pre-anaesthetic assessment, patient optimization, and PACU management. Comprehensive perioperative evaluation and post-operative recovery protocols.

🤖

Robotic Surgery Anaesthesia

Specialized anaesthesia support for robotic surgical procedures. Expertise in positioning, monitoring, and maintaining hemodynamic stability during robotic cases.
