About Scope Anaesthesia Services

Our journey to redefine collaborative perioperative care

Our Story

Scope Anaesthesia Services was founded by five specialist anaesthesiologists in Hyderabad who shared a common vision — to create a collaborative platform that elevates the standard of perioperative care in India. Rather than working in isolation, our founders believed that a group practice model brings together complementary expertise, enables round-the-clock coverage, and fosters continuous learning and quality improvement. Today, Scope partners with leading hospitals to deliver comprehensive anaesthesia, critical care, and emergency services.
