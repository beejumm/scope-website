The Challenge

Hospitals face significant operational challenges when they need verified specialist anaesthesiologists on short notice, especially outside regular working hours. Emergency surgeries often get delayed waiting for the right specialist to arrive. There's no reliable way to quickly identify, verify, and contact available specialists in proximity to the hospital.

The current system relies on manual calls, outdated contact lists, and hope that the specialist can be reached and is available. This delays critical surgeries, compromises patient safety, and creates unnecessary stress for hospital administrators and surgical teams.

How It Works

Six simple steps from request to arrival:

1

Hospital Searches

2

Platform Filters

Safety & Workload Management

Phase 2 Features: Intelligent Workload Controls

⚠️

Doctor Fatigue & Workload Controls

System tracks doctor hours and fatigue levels. Automatic availability suspension after extended shifts. Prevents over-utilisation and ensures doctor safety.

🔒

Concurrent Booking Limits

Prevents a doctor from being double-booked across multiple hospitals. Only one active engagement at a time ensures quality focus and patient safety.

⏳

Smart Cooldown Periods

Mandatory rest periods after emergency surgeries. Graduated cooldown based on case complexity and duration. Ensures doctors are fresh for next case.

🆘

Emergency-Specific Opt-In

Separate availability status for routine vs. emergency cases. Doctors can be unavailable for routine calls but available for true emergencies they're willing to take.

📋

Compliance & Documentation

Automated compliance tracking. Time logs, duty hour monitoring, and documentation ensuring regulatory adherence and professional standards.

🎯

Quality & Performance Metrics

Doctor ratings, response times, and reliability scores. Transparent performance data helps hospitals make informed decisions and maintain service quality.
